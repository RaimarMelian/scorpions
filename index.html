Facts about scorpions

Scorpions are arthropods, they have eight legs, two pedipalps, and a tail with a venom-injecting barb.
Scorpions have two venom glands that produce venom used in hunting and self defense. ... They prey on insects, arthropods and in some cases small vertebrates.

The people are becoming a problem for scorpions though, because a lot of people use their tails to smoke and get "high"

The scorpion's venom is fatal but has rarely killed any human
